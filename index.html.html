Tips

Keep your persona short and focused — this counts as permanent tokens every message. Too much detail and the LLM struggles with it or starts ignoring parts

Avoid describing private parts or anything overtly sexual unless you specifically want the bot to react to it — the LLM will latch onto it and start sexualizing your sona

Scent isn't necessary, but it's fun to include since the bot will mention it during RP

Use {{char}} to reference the bot's name (e.g. "Dislikes: {{char}}") so the persona works across different bots without editing

You can add or remove any field from the templates — they're suggestions, not rules. Tailor it to your RP scenario

If your persona is for a specific bot, include context that matters for that story (family house, relationship to {{char}}, occupation in that world)
